About This Platform

OPEN CALL

OPEN CALL is a free, independent aggregator of international art funding and exhibition opportunities. We collect and curate grants, open calls, residencies, and prizes from institutions worldwide, making them accessible in a single searchable index.

Our mission is to reduce the information asymmetry that exists in the art world — where knowledge of opportunities is often gatekept behind institutional networks, expensive newsletters, or geographic proximity to major art centres.

How it works

Opportunities are sourced from institutional websites, cultural ministry announcements, arts funding body publications, and community submissions. Each listing is manually reviewed for accuracy. Deadlines and details are checked monthly.

Contributing

OPEN CALL relies on the art community to stay comprehensive. If you know of an opportunity not yet listed, use the + Submit tab to add it. All submissions are reviewed within 3–5 business days.

Disclaimer

OPEN CALL is not affiliated with any of the listed organisations. Always verify current deadlines and requirements directly on the funder's official website before applying.
